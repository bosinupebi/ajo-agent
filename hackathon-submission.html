Product Description

Ajo — derived from the Yoruba word for "group or organisational savings" — is a decentralised rotating savings protocol (ROSCA) built on Ethereum. The core is the AjoV1 smart contract suite: a factory that deploys savings pools where groups of people pool ERC-20 tokens and receive payouts at fixed intervals, with smart contracts holding custody and eliminating the need for a trusted intermediary.


Built on top of the AjoV1 contracts is ajo-agent — an autonomous AI agent powered by Claude. The admin creates a pool with a single natural language instruction. Everything after is automatic: a PoolManager background loop watches for member signups, calls addMembers on-chain when the pool fills, then polls every 60 seconds and triggers token payouts in rotation — indefinitely, across restarts, with retry logic and a warning banner for persistent failures. Members join and contribute directly from a browser wallet (MetaMask or compatible), while the agent coordinates all on-chain actions through a self-custodial WDK wallet.
